Meal Suggestion: High-Protein Chicken Wrap Breakfast

A delicious and nutritious breakfast wrap packed with protein to help you gain muscle.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 whole-wheat tortilla
  • 100g grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 50g scrambled eggs (1 large egg)
  • 30g shredded low-fat cheese
  • 50g spinach leaves
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1 tablespoon Greek yogurt

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 400
  • Protein: 35g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fats: 15g
  • Fiber: 7g
