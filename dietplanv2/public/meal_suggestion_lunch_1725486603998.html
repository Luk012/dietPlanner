Meal: Sweet Potato and Black Bean Buddha Bowl

This Sweet Potato and Black Bean Buddha Bowl is a nutrient-dense, flavorful option for your active vegetarian lifestyle. It features roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, quinoa, and a variety of fresh, crunchy vegetables, all topped with a zesty lime dressing. This meal is designed to provide lasting energy and essential nutrients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium sweet potato, diced
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small avocado, sliced
  • 1 small handful of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 450-500 kcal
  • Protein: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Fiber: 15g
