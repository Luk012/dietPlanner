Meal Suggestion: Grilled Tofu and Vegetable Stir-Fry

A protein-rich, vegetarian stir-fry featuring grilled tofu and a medley of fresh vegetables, perfect for building muscle.

Main Ingredients:

  • 200g firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 100g broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • Cooked quinoa or brown rice, for serving

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 8g

How to Prepare:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove from pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add garlic and ginger and saut√© for 1 minute.
  4. Add the carrot, bell pepper, and broccoli. Cook for 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the tofu to the pan. Add soy sauce and stir to combine well.
  6. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
  7. Serve the stir-fry over a portion of cooked quinoa or brown rice.
