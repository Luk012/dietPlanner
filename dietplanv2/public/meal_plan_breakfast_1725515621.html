Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein-Packed Oatmeal with Yogurt

A nutritious and protein-rich breakfast that combines creamy yogurt with oats and nuts to support muscle gain.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oatmeal
  • 1 cup water or milk (depending on preference)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup mixed nuts (e.g., almonds, walnuts, cashews)
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional, for flavor)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 350-400 kcal
  • Protein: Approximately 15-20 grams
  • Carbohydrates: Approximately 45-50 grams
  • Fat: Approximately 10-15 grams
  • Fiber: Approximately 5-7 grams

How to Prepare:

  1. In a saucepan, bring 1 cup of water or milk to a boil.
  2. Add 1/2 cup of oatmeal to the boiling liquid and reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oats are soft and the mixture is thickened.
  3. Remove the saucepan from heat and let the oatmeal cool for a minute.
  4. Transfer the cooked oatmeal to a bowl.
  5. Top the oatmeal with 1/2 cup plain yogurt.
  6. Sprinkle 1/4 cup mixed nuts over the top.
  7. Add 1-2 tablespoons of honey or maple syrup if desired for sweetness.
  8. Optionally, dust with 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon for added flavor.
  9. Mix everything together if preferred, or enjoy as layers.
