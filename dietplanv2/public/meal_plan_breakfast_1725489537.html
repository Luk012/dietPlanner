Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein-Packed Vegetarian Omelette

A delicious and filling vegetarian omelette packed with protein, perfect for muscle gain.

Main Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk (can use plant-based milk)
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup spinach leaves
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (e.g., cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 5g
  • Fat: 25g
  • Fiber: 2g

How to Prepare:

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs and milk together until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the diced bell peppers and tomatoes to the skillet and saut√© for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.
  4. Add the spinach leaves and cook for another minute until wilted.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet, making sure it evenly covers the vegetables.
  6. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for 3-4 minutes, or until the edges start to set.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the omelette.
  8. Using a spatula, gently fold one side of the omelette over the other. Continue cooking for another 2 minutes until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully cooked.
  9. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
