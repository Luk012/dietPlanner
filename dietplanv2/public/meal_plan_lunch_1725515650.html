Your Lunch Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Quinoa and Black Bean Salad

A nutritious and protein-packed vegetarian salad perfect for muscle gain.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup of black beans (cooked or canned, drained and rinsed)
  • 1/2 cup of corn kernels (cooked or canned, drained)
  • 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup of diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup of chopped nuts (like almonds or walnuts)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fiber: 15g
  • Fat: 20g

How to Prepare:

  1. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, cherry tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, avocado, and nuts.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later use. Enjoy your nutritious lunch!
