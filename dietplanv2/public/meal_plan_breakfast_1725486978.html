Meal Suggestion: Avocado Toast with Smoked Salmon

A delicious and nutritious breakfast option that is packed with healthy fats, proteins, and fiber, without any eggs.

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 100 grams of smoked salmon
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Cherry tomatoes and arugula for garnish

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 400 per serving
  • Protein: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 28g
  • Fats: 25g
  • Fiber: 10g

How to Prepare:

  1. Toast the whole-grain bread until golden brown.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toasted bread slices.
  4. Top each slice with smoked salmon.
  5. Optional: Add cherry tomatoes and arugula on top for extra flavor and nutrition.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
