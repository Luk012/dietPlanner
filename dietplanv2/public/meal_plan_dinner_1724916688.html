Your Dinner Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: High-Protein Quinoa and Chickpea Salad

A nutritious and filling salad perfect for muscle gain with a balance of protein, carbs, and healthy fats.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 450 kcal
  • Protein: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 58g
  • Fiber: 12g
  • Fat: 16g

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly under cold water. Cook according to package instructions, usually 15 minutes.
  2. While quinoa is cooking, prepare the vegetables: halve cherry tomatoes, dice cucumber, finely chop red onion, and chop parsley.
  3. Drain and rinse chickpeas.
  4. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and parsley.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss everything together until well mixed.
  7. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese on top before serving.
