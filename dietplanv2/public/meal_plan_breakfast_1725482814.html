Meal Suggestion: Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

A healthy, protein-packed breakfast that's quick and easy to make.

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of black pepper
  • Optional: Sliced cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, or fresh herbs

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 400 kcal
  • Protein: 14g
  • Fats: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 27g
  • Fiber: 10g

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Toast the whole-grain bread slices until they are golden and crisp.
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash it with a fork until smooth.
  3. Bring a pot of water to a simmer. Crack each egg into a small bowl, then gently slip them into the simmering water. Poach for about 3-4 minutes until the whites are set.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toasted bread slices. Season with a pinch of salt and black pepper.
  5. Using a slotted spoon, carefully remove the poached eggs from the water and place one on top of each avocado toast.
  6. Optional: Garnish with sliced cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, or fresh herbs as desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your nutritious breakfast!
