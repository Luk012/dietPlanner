Meal: Mediterranean Chickpea Salad Wrap

This Mediterranean Chickpea Salad Wrap is designed to provide a balanced and nutrient-rich meal for your active, vegetarian lifestyle. It's light yet satisfying, featuring a mix of chickpeas, fresh vegetables, and flavorful herbs wrapped in a whole wheat tortilla. Ideal for keeping you energized through your day.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1 small handful of fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 whole wheat tortilla

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 350-400 kcal
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 10g
