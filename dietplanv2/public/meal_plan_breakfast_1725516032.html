Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein-Packed Vegetarian Oatmeal

A hearty and nutritious oatmeal breakfast designed to meet your muscle-building needs without fruits.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 2 cups milk (or a plant-based milk alternative)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (e.g., almonds, walnuts, or cashews)
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 2 tablespoons protein powder (vegetarian)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional for sweetness)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: ~500 kcal
  • Protein: ~25g
  • Carbohydrates: ~60g
  • Fats: ~15g
  • Fiber: ~8g
  • Calcium: ~40% DV
  • Iron: ~20% DV

How to Prepare:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the milk to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Add the oatmeal to the boiling milk and reduce the heat to a simmer.
  3. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oatmeal is tender and creamy.
  4. Once the oatmeal is cooked, stir in the protein powder until fully combined.
  5. Remove from heat and transfer the oatmeal to a serving bowl.
  6. Top with chopped nuts and chia seeds for added protein and crunch.
  7. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup if you prefer a sweeter taste.
  8. Enjoy your protein-packed oatmeal while it's warm!
