Meal: Protein-Packed Vegetarian Breakfast Bowl

This breakfast bowl is designed for an active lifestyle and a vegetarian diet. It combines a variety of plant-based protein sources, whole grains, and fresh vegetables to provide you with the energy and nutrients you need to start your day.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 1 small handful of spinach or kale
  • 1 tablespoon feta cheese (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 400-450 kcal
  • Protein: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Fiber: 10g
  • Iron: 20% of Daily Value (DV)
  • Calcium: 10% of DV
  • Vitamin C: 25% of DV

Why This Meal:

This Protein-Packed Vegetarian Breakfast Bowl is tailored to fit your active lifestyle and vegetarian diet. Quinoa provides a complete protein, containing all essential amino acids, while black beans contribute additional plant-based protein and fiber, keeping you full and satisfied. Vegetables like cherry tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and greens add essential vitamins and antioxidants. The healthy fats from avocado and olive oil support heart health and overall wellness. This diverse mix ensures you get a balanced, nutrient-dense breakfast to fuel your active mornings.
