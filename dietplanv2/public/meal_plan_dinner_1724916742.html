Your Dinner Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: High-Protein Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry

A delicious and nutritious quinoa and vegetable stir-fry, perfect for gaining muscle.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 block of firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 400
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fats: 15g
  • Fiber: 10g

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Rinse the quinoa well under cold water. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides.
  5. Add the broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, and snap peas. Stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil. Mix well to combine.
  7. Add the cooked quinoa to the vegetable and tofu mixture. Stir to combine and cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
