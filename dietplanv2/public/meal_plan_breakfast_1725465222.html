Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein-Packed Veggie Omelette

A delicious, high-protein vegetarian omelette to help you gain muscle and start your day strong.

Main Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup low-fat milk
  • 1 small bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fats: 25g
  • Fiber: 3g

Preparation Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the diced bell pepper and onion to the skillet and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the mushrooms and spinach to the skillet and cook until the spinach is wilted and the mushrooms are tender, about 3 minutes.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook, stirring gently, until the eggs are just set, about 3-4 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the omelette and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Fold the omelette in half and serve hot.
