Meal Suggestion: Quinoa Salad with Grilled Chicken

A nutritious and delicious quinoa salad paired with succulent grilled chicken, perfect for a balanced lunch.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for dressing)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Fiber: 5g
  • Fat: 20g

How to Prepare:

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water. In a medium pot, bring the 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes or until the water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork and set aside to cool.
  2. Season the chicken breast with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked through. Remove from the grill and let it rest before slicing.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and parsley.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, and minced garlic. Pour the dressing over the quinoa salad and toss to combine.
  6. Slice the grilled chicken and arrange it on top of the quinoa salad.
  7. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later use. Enjoy your nutritious and delicious lunch!
