Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein-Packed Scrambled Eggs with Avocado Toast

A balanced and nutritious breakfast to help you gain muscle by providing high-quality proteins and healthy fats.

Main Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 medium avocado
  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1/4 cup low-fat milk
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes (optional)
  • Fresh chives or parsley for garnish (optional)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 600
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Fat: 35g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Vitamins and minerals: High in Vitamin E, B-Vitamins, and essential fatty acids.
