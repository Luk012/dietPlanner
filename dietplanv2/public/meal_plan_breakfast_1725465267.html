Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: High-Protein Veggie Scramble

A delicious and protein-rich vegetarian breakfast to help you gain muscle.

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup tofu, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/4 cup onions, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric (optional, for color)
  • Whole grain toast or any preferred bread (optional)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: ~350 kcal
  • Protein: ~25g
  • Carbohydrates: ~12g
  • Fats: ~18g
  • Fiber: ~3g

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onions and bell peppers. Saut√© until they are soft, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and add the crumbled tofu to the pan. If using, sprinkle the turmeric over the tofu for color. Cook for another 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Crack the eggs into the pan and stir gently to scramble everything together.
  5. Add the chopped spinach and continue to cook until the spinach is wilted and the eggs are fully cooked.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot, with whole grain toast or your preferred bread on the side.
