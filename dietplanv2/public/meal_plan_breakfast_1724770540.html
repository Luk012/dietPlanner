Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Greek Yogurt with Berries and Nuts

A healthy and balanced breakfast that is low in carbs and high in protein, perfect for managing weight and blood sugar levels.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Greek yogurt (non-fat or low-fat)
  • 1/2 cup of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts)
  • 1 teaspoon of chia seeds
  • Optional: A drizzle of honey or a few drops of vanilla extract

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 250
  • Protein: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 20g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Sugars: 14g (from the berries)
  • Fat: 10g
  • Calcium: 20% of daily value
