Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein-Packed Oatmeal with Fruit

A nutritious and filling breakfast combining protein from meat, calcium from milk, and the natural sweetness of fruits.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup diced meat
  • 1 cup mixed fruits (e.g., berries, sliced banana)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 450
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 55g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 8g

How to Prepare:

  1. In a pot, combine oatmeal and milk. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, for about 5-7 minutes or until the oatmeal thickens.
  2. While the oatmeal is cooking, heat a small pan over medium heat and cook the diced meat until fully cooked and browned.
  3. Once the oatmeal is cooked, mix in the cooked meat.
  4. Top the oatmeal with the mixed fruits.
  5. Drizzle honey over the top if desired.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy your protein-packed breakfast!
