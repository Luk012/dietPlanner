Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein Oatmeal

A high-protein, gluten-free oatmeal to support muscle gain and start your day right.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of gluten-free oatmeal
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 scoop of protein powder (optional, for extra protein)
  • A handful of nuts (avoid if they are part of the dry fruits you dislike)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 400-500 (depending on protein powder and nuts)
  • Protein: 20-30 grams
  • Carbs: 50-60 grams
  • Fat: 10-20 grams
