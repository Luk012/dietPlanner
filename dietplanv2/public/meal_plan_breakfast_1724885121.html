Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: High-Protein Veggie Omelette

A nutritious and protein-packed vegetarian omelette to help you gain muscle and start your day energized.

Main Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (preferably a high-protein cheese)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 350 kcal
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fats: 25g
  • Fiber: 2g

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onions and bell peppers to the pan and saut√© until they are tender.
  4. Add the chopped spinach and tomatoes to the pan and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour the whisked eggs into the pan and let them cook without stirring for a few minutes until the edges start to set.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the omelette.
  7. Fold the omelette in half and cook for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully cooked.
  8. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately.
