Meal: Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry

This Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry is a hearty and nutritious meal tailored to fit your vegetarian lifestyle and dietary restrictions. It leaves out fruits altogether, ensuring it aligns with your dislike for fruits, while still providing a balanced blend of proteins, healthy fats, and carbohydrates for optimal energy and health.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snow peas, trimmed
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce or tamari (for a gluten-free option)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 green onion, finely sliced (optional, for garnish)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: ~400 kcal
  • Protein: ~15 grams
  • Carbohydrates: ~60 grams
  • Fat: ~10 grams
  • Fiber: ~10 grams
  • Vitamins & Minerals: Rich in Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Iron, and Calcium

Why This Meal:

This Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry serves as a nutritious and satisfying meal for an 18-year-old like you with a vegetarian diet and specific dislikes. Quinoa, a complete protein source, supports muscle growth and maintenance, crucial for your age. The diverse vegetables provide a wealth of vitamins, minerals, and fiber essential for overall health and digestive well-being. The olive oil and sesame oil offer healthy fats necessary for brain function. This meal is quick to prepare and versatile, ensuring you stay on track with your dietary preferences and nutritional needs without compromising on flavor and enjoyment.
