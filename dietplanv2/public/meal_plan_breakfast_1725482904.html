Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

A nutritious and delicious breakfast that combines the creamy texture of avocado with the rich protein of poached eggs, perfect for starting your day right.

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Fresh herbs (optional, for garnish)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 350 kcal
  • Protein: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fats: 20g
  • Fiber: 10g

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Toast the slices of whole-grain bread to your desired level of crispiness.
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork, adding salt, pepper, and lemon juice to taste.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto the toasted bread slices.
  5. Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer and add a dash of vinegar (optional) to help the eggs hold their shape.
  6. Poach the eggs by cracking them into individual bowls and sliding them gently into the simmering water. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
  7. Using a slotted spoon, remove the poached eggs and place one on top of each avocado toast.
  8. Season with additional salt and pepper and garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy!
