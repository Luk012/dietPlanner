Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

A nutritious and balanced breakfast to help you lose weight while providing essential nutrients for your gym workouts.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 whole grain bread slice
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • A handful of baby spinach (optional)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: ~300
  • Protein: ~15g
  • Carbohydrates: ~20g
  • Fats: ~20g
  • Fiber: ~7g
  • Vitamins and Minerals: High in vitamins A, E, K, and B6, and minerals like potassium and magnesium
