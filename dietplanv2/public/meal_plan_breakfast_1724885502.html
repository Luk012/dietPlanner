Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein-Packed Oatmeal

A nutritious and filling oatmeal blend enhanced with milk and fruits to kickstart your muscle gain journey.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oat flakes
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 banana (or any fruit you don't mind)
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter (optional, for additional protein)
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 400-450 kcal
  • Protein: Approximately 15-20 grams
  • Carbohydrates: Approximately 60-70 grams
  • Fats: Approximately 10 grams
  • Fiber: Approximately 8 grams

Preparation Instructions:

  1. In a small pot, bring 2 cups of milk to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Add 1 cup of oat flakes to the simmering milk, stirring occasionally.
  3. Let the mixture cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the oats are soft and the mixture thickens.
  4. While the oatmeal is cooking, slice your banana (or chosen fruit) into small pieces.
  5. Once the oatmeal is ready, pour it into a bowl and top with the sliced fruit.
  6. Optionally, add 1 tablespoon of peanut butter and 1 teaspoon of honey or maple syrup for additional flavor and protein.
  7. Mix well and enjoy your hearty, protein-packed oatmeal breakfast!
