Your Lunch Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burger

A delicious and hearty vegetarian burger packed with protein to help you gain muscle.

Main Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 4 whole wheat burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional, can use plant-based cheese)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 tablespoons hummus
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: ~400 kcal per serving
  • Protein: ~20g per serving
  • Carbohydrates: ~35g per serving
  • Fats: ~18g per serving
  • Fiber: ~8g per serving

How to Prepare:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Clean the portobello mushrooms and remove the stems.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the mixture onto both sides of the portobello mushrooms.
  5. Place the mushrooms on the grill, gill-side down first, for about 4-5 minutes per side or until tender.
  6. If using cheese, place a slice on each mushroom during the last minute of grilling to melt.
  7. Toast the whole wheat buns on the grill for a minute until lightly browned.
  8. Spread a tablespoon of hummus on the bottom half of each bun.
  9. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled portobello mushroom on top of the hummus, followed by avocado slices, spinach leaves, tomato slices, and red onion.
  10. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
