Meal Suggestion: Veggie Omelette with Whole Grain Toast

A protein-packed vegetarian breakfast to help you gain muscle.

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional for extra protein)
  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 tbsp olive oil for cooking
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
  • Fats: 20g
  • Fiber: 8g

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped vegetables (bell peppers, spinach, tomatoes, onions) and sauté until they are softened.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the sautéed vegetables in the pan and cook until the eggs are set.
  5. Optional: Sprinkle shredded cheese on top and allow it to melt.
  6. Toast the whole grain bread slices until they are golden brown.
  7. Serve the veggie omelette with the toasted whole grain bread on the side.
  8. Enjoy your muscle-gaining breakfast!
