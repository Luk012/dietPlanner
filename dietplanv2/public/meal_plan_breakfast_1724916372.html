Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: High-Protein Oatmeal

A nutritious and quick breakfast designed to help you gain muscle, tailored to your vegetarian diet and nutritional needs.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of oatmeal
  • 1.5 cups of milk (or a plant-based alternative)
  • 1 scoop of protein powder (vanilla or unflavored)
  • 1 tablespoon of chia seeds
  • Optional: A small amount of fruit you can tolerate, chopped (e.g., a handful of berries)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 400 kcal
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fats: 10g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Sugar: Minimal (depending on fruit added)

Preparation Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the oatmeal and milk over medium heat.
  2. Stir occasionally, and bring to a gentle boil.
  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat and simmer for about 5-7 minutes until the oatmeal is cooked and reaches the desired consistency.
  4. Stir in the protein powder and chia seeds until well mixed.
  5. If adding fruit, mix in the chopped fruit at this stage.
  6. Remove from heat and let it sit for a minute to thicken.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your high-protein oatmeal!
