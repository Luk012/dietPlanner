Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein Oatmeal

A nutritious and protein-rich breakfast to help you gain muscle, using simple ingredients available in your fridge.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of oatmeal
  • 2 cups of milk
  • Nuts or seeds (e.g., almonds, chia seeds) for protein boost

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approximately 300 kcal
  • Protein: Approximately 12g
  • Carbohydrates: Approximately 40g
  • Fats: Approximately 10g
  • Fiber: Approximately 5g

How to Prepare:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the oatmeal with milk over medium heat.
  2. Stir occasionally and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and let it simmer for about 5-10 minutes, until the oatmeal is tender and the mixture thickens.
  4. Remove from heat and pour into a bowl.
  5. Top with nuts or seeds to add extra protein and enhance the flavor.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy your muscle-gain breakfast!
