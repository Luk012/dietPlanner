Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Protein Oatmeal

A nutritious and muscle-building vegetarian breakfast to start your day right.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 1 cup milk (or a plant-based milk alternative)
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (optional, check for vegetarian-friendly)
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, etc.)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 500 kcal
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 8g

How to Prepare:

  1. In a pot, combine 1 cup of oatmeal and 1 cup of milk (or plant-based milk). Bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oats are cooked and the mixture is thick.
  3. Stir in 1 scoop of vanilla protein powder (ensure it's vegetarian-friendly), mixing well to combine.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of peanut butter and stir until it is fully melted and incorporated.
  5. Top with a handful of chopped nuts for added crunch and protein.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy your muscle-building breakfast!
