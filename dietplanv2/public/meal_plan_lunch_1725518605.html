Meal Suggestion: Grilled Tofu and Vegetable Wrap

A delicious and protein-packed vegetarian wrap that will help you with muscle gain while adhering to your dietary restrictions.

Main Ingredients:

  • 200g firm tofu
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small zucchini, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 4 whole wheat tortillas
  • 50g spinach leaves
  • 50g hummus
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 450 kcal
  • Protein: 26g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 12g

How to Prepare:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Press the tofu to remove excess water and cut it into small cubes.
  3. In a bowl, mix olive oil, soy sauce, garlic powder, and paprika. Add the tofu cubes and let them marinate for 10 minutes.
  4. Grill the tofu cubes for about 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown.
  5. In the meantime, grill the sliced red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, zucchini, and red onion until tender.
  6. Warm the whole wheat tortillas on the grill for about 1-2 minutes.
  7. Spread hummus on each tortilla, add a handful of spinach leaves, grilled vegetables, and tofu cubes.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Roll up the tortillas to make wraps, and they're ready to serve!
