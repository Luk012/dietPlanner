Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: High-Protein Omelette with Oats and Fruit

This meal is designed to help you gain muscle by providing a high amount of protein along with essential vitamins and minerals to support your active lifestyle.

Main Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 50g spinach
  • 30g low-fat cheese (such as feta or mozzarella)
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup milk (or a dairy-free alternative)
  • 1 banana
  • Handful of mixed berries (e.g., blueberries, strawberries)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 600-650 kcal
  • Protein: 35-40g
  • Carbohydrates: 60-70g
  • Fats: 20-25g
  • Fiber: 10g
  • Vitamins: A, C, D, B6, B12
  • Minerals: Iron, Calcium, Magnesium, Potassium
