Meal Suggestion: Grilled Tofu and Vegetable Stir-Fry

A protein-rich, vegetarian meal perfect for muscle gain, featuring grilled tofu and a variety of vegetables for balanced nutrition.

Main Ingredients:

  • 200g firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1/2 cup quinoa (dry)
  • 1 cup water (for quinoa)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 10g

How to Prepare:

  1. Cook quinoa: Rinse 1/2 cup of quinoa under cold water. In a pot, add 1 cup of water and quinoa, bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cover. Cook for about 15 minutes or until water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add cubed tofu to the pan, and cook until it is golden brown on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add chopped bell pepper, sliced carrot, and broccoli florets to the pan. Stir-fry for about 5 minutes until vegetables are tender but crisp.
  5. Pour soy sauce over the tofu and vegetables, stirring to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the stir-fry, then serve it over a bed of cooked quinoa.
