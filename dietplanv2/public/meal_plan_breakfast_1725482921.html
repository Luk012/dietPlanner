Meal Suggestion: Avocado Toast with Smoked Salmon

A nutritious, egg-free breakfast option that is packed with healthy fats and protein.

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 50g smoked salmon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes, cherry tomatoes, fresh dill

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Sodium: 400mg

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Toast the 2 slices of whole-grain bread until golden brown.
  2. While the bread is toasting, halve the avocado, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork and mix in 1 tbsp of olive oil, 1 tbsp of lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto the toasted bread slices.
  5. Top each slice with 25g of smoked salmon.
  6. Optionally, garnish with red pepper flakes, cherry tomatoes, or fresh dill.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
