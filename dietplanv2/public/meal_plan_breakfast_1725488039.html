Your Breakfast Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Fruity Oatmeal with a Side of Protein

A wholesome oatmeal breakfast is complemented with fresh fruits and a serving of protein to keep you energized throughout the morning.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 2 cups milk (or any non-dairy alternative)
  • 1/2 cup sliced fresh fruits (e.g., berries, banana, apple)
  • 3 oz cooked meat (e.g., chicken, turkey, or lean beef)
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: Approx. 400 kcal
  • Protein: Approx. 28g
  • Carbohydrates: Approx. 50g
  • Fiber: Approx. 8g
  • Fat: Approx. 10g
  • Calcium: Approx. 30% of daily value

How to Prepare:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the milk to a gentle boil.
  2. Add the oatmeal to the saucepan, reduce the heat, and let it simmer for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Once the oatmeal is cooked, remove it from the heat and let it sit for a minute to thicken.
  4. In the meantime, slice your chosen fresh fruits.
  5. Serve the oatmeal in a bowl, top with sliced fruits, and drizzle with honey or maple syrup if desired.
  6. On a separate plate, serve the cooked meat as a side to complement your oatmeal.
  7. Enjoy your balanced and nutritious breakfast!
