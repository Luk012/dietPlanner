Meal Suggestion: Lentil and Chickpea Salad with Quinoa

A nutritious and protein-packed salad perfect for muscle gain, featuring lentils, chickpeas, and quinoa.

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup toasted nuts (optional, for added crunch)

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 450 kcal
  • Protein: 22g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Fiber: 15g
  • Vitamin C: 50% of Daily Value (DV)
  • Iron: 40% of DV

How to Prepare:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine cooked lentils, chickpeas, and quinoa.
  2. Add diced cucumber, bell peppers, red onion, and fresh parsley.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad and mix well to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Optional: Sprinkle toasted nuts over the top for added crunch.
  6. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 days for a refreshing and nutritious lunch.
