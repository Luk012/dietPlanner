Meal Suggestion: Smoked Salmon Avocado Toast

A delicious and nutritious breakfast option featuring smoked salmon and creamy avocado on whole-grain toast.

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 100g smoked salmon
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
  • Chopped chives or dill for garnish

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 20g
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Fiber: 8g

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Toast the slices of whole-grain bread until golden brown.
  2. In a bowl, mash the ripe avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on the toasted bread.
  4. Layer the smoked salmon slices on top of the avocado.
  5. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil.
  6. Garnish with chopped chives or dill and serve immediately.
