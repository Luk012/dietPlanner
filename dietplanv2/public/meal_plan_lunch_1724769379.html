Your Lunch Meal Plan

Meal Suggestion: Grilled Chicken Quinoa Bowl

A nutritious and filling meal designed to help you gain muscle. This bowl is packed with protein, healthy carbs, and essential nutrients to fuel your active lifestyle.

Main Ingredients:

  • 150g grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro for garnish

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories: 600 kcal
  • Protein: 45g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Rich in Vitamin C, Vitamin A, Calcium, and Iron
